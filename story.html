About Us

Welcome to Sheik Coconuts — a family business built on tradition, trust, and quality for three generations.

For decades, our family has been deeply connected to coconut farming and procurement. What started as a traditional farming and trading business has grown into a dedicated enterprise that brings the goodness of coconut to customers through a wide range of premium products.

We have our own coconut farms and a strong procurement network, which helps us ensure consistent quality, freshness, and reliability in every product we offer. With years of experience and deep knowledge of the industry, we carefully handle every stage — from sourcing fresh coconuts to processing valuable coconut-based products.

The Craft

What We Do

At Sheik Coconuts, we manufacture and supply a variety of coconut products and by-products, including:

  • Coconut Oil
  • Tender Coconut / Coconut Water / Coconut
  • Dried Coconut Products
  • Coconut Pith
  • Coconut Coir
  • Coconut Fibre

Our focus is on making the best use of every part of the coconut, turning nature’s gift into useful, high-quality products for different industries and everyday needs.
